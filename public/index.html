reSearch: the paper recommendation app

Brian Lin, Thomas Ng, Robert Shelansky, Adrian Bivol

reSearch is an app that helps scientists find relevant papers using a recommendation system. Enter a search query, and you'll have the opportunity to tell us whether the results are relevant or not. Build a station that learns your preferences and subscribe to updates in your field.
